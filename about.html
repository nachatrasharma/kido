We Create New

Ch. Vedram School is a prestigious new primary school which aims to pre revolutionize the world of education. Launched by Ch. Vedram, this private organization endeavours to provide quality education to the future generations of this country.


Ch. Vedram School is committed towards imparting academic excellence along with intellectual growth, art, athletics, high standards of ethical awareness, sportsmanship, and community service. The school upholds the founders commitment to excellence in all fields, with emphasis on the belief that each child is a treasure house


The school makes sure that every single student and family feels welcome and understood on equal terms.

DIRECTOR'S MESSAGE

As a Director of this prestigious school my aim is to effectively ensure high standards of academic excellence in well- rounded and holistic educational programmes.
